Inflatables
Week 1
Step 1
Make design file in Illustrator. File should contains 2 shapes: one for baking paper and for vinyl. Dont forget to make space for place how you are going to blow air inside your work.
Step 2
Export file in format .dxf
Step 3
Open file in Silhuette.
Step 4
Go to Design/ Page set up / change artboard to the size of printing machine A4, portrait.
Step 5
Put on the art board shape for baking paper
Step 6
Put baking paper to the special board for printing. It should stick very well to the board otherwise printer might tear the paper
Step 7
Put board in the printer.
Step 8
Go to Send and adjust settings: For backing paper:Speed - 2, Force - 10
Step 9
Change setting on printer - element for cutting - for the chosen speed in program.
Step 10
Make a test. If it works well press Send
Step 11
When job is done put on the printing board piece of vinyl. Important! Vinyl should be put with matte side on the top.
Step 12
On the computer put to the artboard shape for vinyl.
Step 13
Go to Send and adjust settings. For Vinyl: Material - vinyl, matte. Speed - 4. Force - 15
Step 14
Make a test. If it works well press Send and print. You will need two similar vinyl pieces.
Step 15
When vilyl are cutted you have to separate cutted pieces from the rest and from the foil.
Step 16
Make a sendwich - vinyl - baking paper - vilyl.
Step 17
Press. In the pressing machine put your sandwich. Important! Before press you should put baking paper under and above your sandwich - otherwise mashine will melt vinyl.
Step 18
Hot press for 15 seconds. Take out, wait for a few seconds to cool it down and then separate your shape from the foil.
Step 19
Put in the airhole tube and test it
Step 20
Congratulations - your inflatable shape is done! :)
