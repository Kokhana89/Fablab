Final Project
First idea
At the beginning I wanted to make a thermochromic cover and work with thermochromic powder. But during the tests I realized that this idea will not work, because the heat created with conductive thread was not big enough to make ink change it color.
TESTED: different patterns, materials and different combination of thermochromic pigment and paint, glue. RESULT: does not heat enough to make thermochromic ink work.
